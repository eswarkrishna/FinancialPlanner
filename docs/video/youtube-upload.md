FinancialPlanner is a free, open-source financial planning tool that helps you compare loan payoff strategies, model prepayments, plan for unemployment with PF/401(k) withdrawals, and track your budget — all in your browser.

🔗 Try it free: https://eswarkrishna.github.io/FinancialPlanner/

• Multi-debt payoff planner (avalanche vs snowball)
• Retirement corpus projection with conservative/optimistic scenarios

• 50/30/20 budget planner with emergency fund runway
